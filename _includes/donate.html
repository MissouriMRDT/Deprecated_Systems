Donation Instructions

Becoming a sponsor is very simple and can be accomplished in one of two ways:

Option 1 - Mail

Sending a check is as easy as making it out to Missouri S&T with Mars Rover Design Team in the memo line. The checks can then be mailed to:

Susan Robison
Administrative Assistant
Student Design and Experiential Learning Center
Missouri University of Science & Technology
1051 N. Bishop
116 Kummer Student Design Center
Rolla, Missouri 65409-1410

Option 2 - Online

To make a donation online, please visit: givingtomst.missouri.edu
The form can be filled out using the following steps:

  1. Select the 'Miners by Design' option from drop-down selection
  2. Enter the amount of the donation
  3. Enter 'Mars Rover Design Team' into the text dialogue
  4. Continue to payment information
